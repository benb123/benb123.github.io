Chorduroy

A Web app that combines the Guitar fretboard and Piano keyboard allowing for realistic chords, arpeggios and rythm using MIDI.

Suitable for those learning music or just wanting to work out different chords anywhere on the fretboard.

Works with MIDI keyboards or mouse/computer keyboard shortcuts.

Try it out using Apple's Garage Band (TM) as a Software based Synth or feed it out to real MIDI modules.
